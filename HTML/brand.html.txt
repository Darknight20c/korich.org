    <h2>Typography</h2>
    <p>Our primary font is Arial for body text, paired with Helvetica for headings. Use bold weights for emphasis and maintain consistent sizing across platforms.</p>

    <h2>Brand Voice</h2>
    <p>Korich’s voice is professional, confident, and approachable. We communicate with clarity, positivity, and respect, reflecting our commitment to excellence and community.</p>

    <h2>Logo Usage Guidelines</h2>
    <ul>
      <li>Maintain clear space around the logo equal to the height of the “K”.</li>
      <li>Do not alter colors, proportions, or orientation.</li>
      <li>Use only approved versions on official materials.</li>
      <li>Ensure logos are legible on all backgrounds.</li>
    </ul>

    <h2>Brand Applications</h2>
    <p>Our brand elements are applied consistently across packaging, signage, digital platforms, and promotional materials to reinforce recognition and trust.</p>
